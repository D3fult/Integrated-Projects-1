STUDENT LIFE


You don't just join a university when you enroll at ALU; you join a group of lifelong learners. There’s always an opportunity to grow and have fun!

"Learning at ALU extends beyond the classroom, be it rehearsing a play for the annual ArtsFest, leading the soccer team during an ALU Alive #Move session, or gearing up to take center stage and host a community gathering. There’s always an opportunity to grow and have fun!"

Vision



Our vision is to enable our students to embark on a holistic journey in which they take charge and own their development and growth towards becoming well-rounded leaders at ALU and beyond.

Mission



Our mission is to empower students to co-design rigorous and high-quality services centered on student support and growth, keep the campus safe, well-run, and provide interaction and positive culture-building opportunities.

The Student Life Framework

Student Life at ALU focuses on the holistic student experience and is built on a foundation of four pillars: Support, Development, Safety, and Culture. All our offerings and programs draw from these pillars to ensure that our students have a well-rounded, meaningful, and balanced university experience.

The Student Life Team invests in
non-academic learning for students, contributing to the ALU 21st Century Skills objective.

As an ALU student, you have access to training & coaching sessions and upskilling programmes through the team. These sessions aim to help you develop the ability to self-regulate (organize & manage your resources & responsibilities) and work efficiently. ALU hosts a series of activities designed to foster community and showcase students’ diverse and exceptional talent. Monthly community lunches, assemblies, and annual student-run activities like ArtsFest and Wellness Week - all held during the academic year.

A Day in The Life of An ALU Student

In today’s highly competitive market, the need for professionals with strong interpersonal & social skills is crucial and provides a distinct competitive edge. As a result, the Student Life Team has set out to make all avenues accessible to help in practical readiness, thus fulfilling these demands and within selected places to serve within your community. With that said, ready to get started?

By 2035, ALU aims to train three million ethical and entrepreneurial leaders for Africa and the rest of the world. It employs a personalized, student-driven, project-based, and mission-oriented approach to creating agile, lifelong learners capable of adapting to a changing world.
