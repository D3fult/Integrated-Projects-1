Two Different Countries,
One Amazing Experience.



Africa is home to some of the most diverse people on the planet, both within and across its countries, making it a land rich in untapped opportunities. ALU offers an experience designed to address one of the continent’s greatest challenges - accessible education that develops ethical and entrepreneurial leaders.

Therefore, ALU's Undergraduate Degrees are designed to provide students with an organized educational experience. Students begin with the Leadership Core, where they acquire essential practical skills that will assist them in their professional settings. Following that, they progress to their Degree Programs, where they pursue their selected majors.

ALU Campuses Offer:



  • An innovative learning experience driven by the seven meta-skills we believe is sought out by top employers across the globe. In our Leadership Core, you will practice and master these skills.

  • A selection of degree programmes supplemented by our African Studies course.

  • A pan-African experience that allows you to network and establish relationships with some of the continent's brightest, most enthusiastic, and motivated peers and leaders.

  • A robust ALUAlive Programme to help you create a meaningful Student Life experience for yourself that is full of growth, discovery, and ownership.
