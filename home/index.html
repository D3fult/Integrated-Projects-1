Our Locations

Mauritius



ALC's main campus was established in early 2018 in Pamplemousses, Mauritius' northern region, midway between the capital city, Port Louis, and the lively expat district of Grand Baie.

Our students may immerse themselves in a diverse culture with historical roots in Asia, Africa, and Europe, all while living on a tropical island that is frequently recognized as one of Africa’s safest and most education-driven countries.

Rwanda



The new ALU campus is located in Bumbogo, Kigali Innovation City, poised to become Africa's centre of excellence in education. Kigali City, Rwanda's capital, is a vibrant and well-organised city boasting a diverse community. Hosting various restaurants, bars, clubs, sports clubs, and cafés, Kigali offers limitless entertainment for ALU students.

The campus has three buildings with two floors each — Social Commons, Enterprise Commons, and Learning Commons, joined by a covered pathway called “Collab Street.” With a 6,500 sqm built area, the campus has 21 classrooms and pods distributed across the campus for peer and self-work.

Discover Our University

By 2035, ALU aims to train three million ethical and entrepreneurial leaders for Africa and the rest of the world. It employs a personalized, student-driven, project-based, and mission-oriented approach to creating agile, lifelong learners capable of adapting to a changing world.
